Why ‘After the flood’?

We help companies that are drowning in data.

Since 2011 we have been working with organisations who can see the potential in their data but need help making it tangible.

We also work with companies who have definite commercial targets and need help in sourcing the right data to meet those goals.

What we do

We create new business value by making new customer experiences.

We create new products and services from complex data, creating ownership capacity and commercial strategy to embed this value creation within the business.

We partner with clients like Ford Smart Mobility, Nikkei, the UK Government Digital Service, BBC, UEFA, and Google.

We are a creative, experienced, and cross-functional team that customises every project to the organisation’s needs to maximise long-term value.

Our approach

We are a design consultancy. This means we make sure we are asking the right question and that we make the solution tangible as soon as possible.

Projects are split into three phases:

  • Agreeing on the value to be created: problem definition and agreeing a proposal
  • Validating tangible services: research, synthesis, prototyping and testing
  • Creating lasting value: helping build ownership capacity and commercial strategy
